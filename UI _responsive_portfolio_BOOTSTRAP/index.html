We believe you should not have to sacrifice quality for affordability. Visit our showrooms in Dallas and Frisco to shop our designs from Italy, Asia, Europe, Canada and the United States. Our mission is to bring you contemporary styling for a comfortable life.

Zuri Furniture has put together an imaginative collection targeting style-conscious buyers. Blending contemporary styles, modern lines and comfortable appeal, we have brought to Texas one of the largest and most unique selections of furniture and decor for the home and office. In addition, Zuri carries modern art, classic accessories, brilliant area rugs, unique sculptures and elegant fragrances.
